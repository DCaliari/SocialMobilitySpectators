The parents of each of the four members of the society have status: high (green) or low (red).

There are four colored balls with a different number of stars representing the members of the society.

The stars represent the results in the task you have previously performed. “Two stars” indicate that the member is in the top half of the results in the task, “one star” indicate that the member is in the bottom half.

For each parent’s status there is always one “two stars” and one “one star” ball.

This means that the parent’s status does not give you any information regarding yours and others’ results in the task.
