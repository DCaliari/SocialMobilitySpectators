The study consists of an EFFORT TASK followed by a MAIN TASK with a series of three questions about how to assign the bonus of 2 dollars.


EFFORT TASK:


The EFFORT TASK is a contest among all participants and serves the purpose of dividing participants into high performers and low performers.

The contest works as follows:

You will see a series of screens with two 10x10 matrices of two-digits numbers.

Your task is to find the maximum number in the first and the second matrix, sum them, and write the answer in the box below the matrices [see an example below].

You are allowed to continue to the next set of matrices only if you give the correct answer.

The task will last 3 minutes and the objective is to complete as many screens as possible.

At the end of the task, all participants will be ranked according to their performance. The top 50% will be assigned to the high performers, and the bottom 50% will be assigned to the low performers.

Being high- or low-performers will be used in the MAIN TASK when deciding how to assing the bonuses. You will receive further instructions after the effort task.

Clicking “CONTINUE” you will start the task.
